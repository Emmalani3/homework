What We Do

Whiting's Ceramic Club is a group for ceramic artists and potters. This club sdesigned to help members gain access to tools to create and fire pottery andgice members an opportunity to make friends with he same interests, learn form eachothers experience and grow their skills.

Join Us As A Member

Join the club and get access to our skills and tools! Membership costs $90 a year and gives you access to unlimited work space hours, wheel time and materials at costs from our lab stock! Get listed in our artist database and get comissions.

Get Commissions

We list members in our artist database to help you get your work noticed. You can choose to be listed as a working artist and commission requests will be passed along to you. This is a free service we offer all artists! There are no costs WCS just gives you an opportunity to start the conversation.
